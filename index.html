Epic Game Night

Epic Game Night is hosted in the computer science hallway (currently Corridor 13, rooms 111-116).
Time: 4:30 pm to about 9:30 pm
Tickets:
  • $5 in advance
  • $6 the day of
  • $7 at the door
  • FREE for the first 20 with a Nintendo Switch + Smash Ultimate

  10. Stage Morph: Off

  12. Team Attack: On
  13. Launch Rate: 1.0x
  14. Underdog Boost: Off
  15. Score Display: Off

  17. Custom Balance: Off
  18. Echo Fighters: Separate
  19. Radar: Small
